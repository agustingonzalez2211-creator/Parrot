01 — Design Philosophy
Principles
01
Raw & Precise
Every element justifies its existence. Sharp 2px corners, monospace type, and zero decorative noise communicate technical intent — the aesthetic of the machine.
02
Signal Over Style
Color communicates state, never decoration. Violet structures the interface. Red marks recording and danger. Green confirms success. If the color has no semantic role, remove it.
03
Motion with Purpose
Animations guide attention and confirm state changes — never entertain. Entrance sequences use expo.out. Micro-transitions stay under 200ms. Gratuitous motion is noise.
